The story behind Xiaojia

My Journey

I started in Mechanical Engineering, but I was always drawn to visual storytelling and human-centered problem solving. That curiosity led me toward communication design.

I moved to Canada to study at Emily Carr University, where I built a foundation across branding, motion, illustration, and digital product design.

Since then, I have worked with startups and larger teams including Tencent, learning how design can connect people, clarify ideas, and make experiences feel thoughtful and alive.
